How It Works

Enter your total cash and split it across three time-based tranches. The app scores each ETF in our curated dataset on yield, total expense ratio (TER), credit quality, and average daily volume — then recommends the highest-scoring fund per tranche.

30 Days
Capital preservation priority. Ultra-short T-bills and money market ETFs. Credit quality weighted highest.
90 Days
Balanced yield and safety. Short-duration Treasury and investment-grade bond ETFs.
180 Days+
Yield optimisation. Short-to-medium duration corporate and government bond ETFs.
